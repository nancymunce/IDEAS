SPC Instructional Support Structure


The 10-Second Test
If the question is:

“How do I technically do this?” → ATST
“What should I design and why?” → IDEAS

ATST

ATST is your Canvas + instructional technology support crew (tickets + 1:1 help) for “how do I do this in Canvas / why isn’t this working / how do I set this up,” including technical support for Zoom and other instructional-tech tools.

Getting into Canvas, access, accounts, and “why can’t I see…?”

  • “I can’t log into Canvas — what do I do?”
  • “My course isn’t showing on my Dashboard.”
  • “I’m listed as a teacher in Banner/MySPC but I don’t have access in Canvas.”
  • “A student says they can’t access the course / it disappeared.”
  • “I can’t access Canvas from home / it keeps looping / blank screen.”
  • “Canvas is slow / spinning wheel / pages won’t load.”
  • “Which browser should I use? Do I need to clear cache/cookies?”
  • “How do I use the Help/Support button inside Canvas to get help or find guides?”

IDEAS

IDEAS is the team responsible for learning design and course strategy. If the question is about learning design, alignment, engagement, pedagogy, quality, structure, course improvement, or instructional strategy, it belongs to IDEAS. IDEAS supports the design and improvement of the learning experience within state-approved curriculum.
