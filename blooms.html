Exploring Bloom's Taxonomy

What is Bloom's Taxonomy?

Bloom's Taxonomy is a hierarchical framework used to classify educational learning objectives into levels of complexity and specificity. It helps educators design effective learning experiences and assessments that promote higher-order thinking skills.

Click on any level of the pyramid below to explore its description, key verbs, learning objectives, and example activities.

Why Use Bloom's Taxonomy?

Bloom's Taxonomy provides a valuable framework for educators to design effective learning experiences and assessments. By understanding the different levels of cognitive skills, educators can create learning objectives and activities that promote higher-order thinking and deeper understanding, preparing students for success in a complex and ever-changing world.
